    5 WARNINGS AND PRECAUTIONS



  EXCERPT:   Infusion reactions may occur. Premedicate with diphenhydramine. Slow or interrupt infusion and administer treatment based on severity of the reaction. (  5.1  )



 



   5.1 Infusion Reactions



  Infusion-related reactions were reported during administration of raxibacumab in clinical trials including reports of rash, urticaria, and pruritus. If these reactions occur, slow or interrupt raxibacumab infusion and administer appropriate treatment based on severity of the reaction.



 Premedicate with diphenhydramine within 1 hour prior to administering raxibacumab to reduce the risk of infusion reactions [see Dosage and Administration (  2.1  ), Adverse Reactions (  6.1  )].
